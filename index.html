SB LIV Props

The Super Bowl is the biggest sporting event of the year in the US, and the sports books offer an outrageous number of wagers you can make. They include important things like who will win and less important things like what will be the first letter in the name of the MVP of The Puppy Bowl. Each one is called a "prop".

Since these details are of crucial importance, I organize a contest every year to see who can correctly predict the most of them. I weight each outcome based on its likelihood -- according to the odds from the sportsbook.

Prizes are not necessary for a contest of this magnitude.
